With all forms of transmission medium (electrical or optical) there is a short time delay for the signal to travel (propagate) through the medium. For instance:
  • In free space speed approximates to 3*108 ms-1
  • In cables speed approximates to 2*108 ms-1
Transmission delay, on the other hand, is the time taken to transmit a block or frame at the specified bit rate. With this information, one can derive a simple formula for calculating the overall delay:
delay = n r transmission delay + d v propagation delay
